# Screen: Welcome & Value Proposition
**ID:** ONB-01
**Parent Flow:** Onboarding

## 1. Purpose
To introduce the user to the core philosophy of RunningCoach ("Evidence-based," "Adaptive," "Injury Prevention") and drive them to start the setup process.

## 2. Layout & Structure
*   **Type:** Full-screen Carousel / Slider.
*   **Navigation:**
    *   **Bottom:** Page indicators (dots).
    *   **Footer:** "Get Started" button (Primary) and "Log In" (Secondary/Ghost).

## 3. UI Components
### 3.1. Carousel Slides
*   **Slide 1:**
    *   **Image/Graphic:** Abstract dynamic visualization of a runner or data points connecting.
    *   **Headline:** "Train Smarter, Not Harder."
    *   **Subtext:** "Evidence-based training that adapts to your biology and life schedule."
*   **Slide 2:**
    *   **Image/Graphic:** Traffic light system visualization (Green/Yellow/Red).
    *   **Headline:** "Listen to Your Body."
    *   **Subtext:** "Daily readiness checks ensure you never overtrain or risk injury."
*   **Slide 3:**
    *   **Image/Graphic:** Calendar adapting (shifting blocks).
    *   **Headline:** "Life Happens."
    *   **Subtext:** "Missed a run? Sick? Traveling? The engine automatically adjusts your plan."

### 3.2. Actions
*   **Button:** `Primary Button` (Electric Blue Gradient)
    *   **Label:** "Get Started"
    *   **Action:** Navigate to `ONB-02` (Baseline Data).
*   **Button:** `Ghost Button`
    *   **Label:** "I already have an account"
    *   **Action:** Open Login Modal.

## 4. Dynamic Data
*   None (Static Content).

## 5. Design System References
*   **Typography:** H1 for Headlines (32px), Body for Subtext (16px).
*   **Colors:** Background `Void Black` (#050505). Text `Primary Text` (#F8FAFC).
*   **Animation:** Smooth slide transitions. Elements fade in/up on slide change.
